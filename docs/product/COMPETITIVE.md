# Canon — Competitive Research

**What this is:** Notes on how Canon compares to existing tools in the learning and curriculum space. Not a formal teardown — a working document. Updated as I encounter new products or think through the positioning more carefully.

**Why it's here:** Canon is solving a specific problem (self-directed learning with output, not just consumption) in a space full of adjacent tools that look similar but aren't. Knowing exactly where Canon differs — and where it doesn't — sharpens product decisions.

---

## How to read this

Each section covers a category of tools, not individual products. Within each category: what they do well, where they fall short, and what Canon does differently.
